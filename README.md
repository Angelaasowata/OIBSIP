# OIBSIP
Retail Sales EDA: Data Cleaning, Visualization, Trend Analysis &amp; Business Insights using Python.
## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Project Objectives
- Clean and inspect the dataset
- Analyze sales trends
- Explore customer demographics
- Identify top performing products
- Generate actionable business insights

## Key Visualizations
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Quarterly Sales Trend
- Customer Demographics
- Top 10 Best Selling Products
- Revenue by Product Category
- Correlation Heatmap

## Business Recommendations

## Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high selling products to meet customer demand.
- Promote low selling products through discounts and marketing campaigns.
- Use customer demographics to create targeted marketing strategies.
- Monitor sales trends regularly to improve inventory planning.
- Maintain clean and accurate data for better business decisions.
